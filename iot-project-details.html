Lead Product Designer

In my role as the Lead Product Designer, I was at the forefront of hardware innovation. My primary responsibility was to translate conceptual ideas into tangible, functional prototypes that were both user-friendly and manufacturable.

This involved end-to-end mechanical design using SolidWorks, where I designed and iterated on custom enclosures to house our electronic components. I worked in close collaboration with the electrical engineering team to ensure seamless PCB integration, optimizing for space, thermal management, and ease of assembly.

Key Contributions:

  • Led the mechanical design and prototyping of the product casing from concept to pre-production.
  • Utilized FDM and SLA 3D printing for rapid prototyping, allowing for quick iteration and functional testing.
  • Collaborated on PCB layout to ensure proper fit, port alignment, and function within the enclosure.
  • Managed component sourcing and established relationships with manufacturers for small-batch production runs.
