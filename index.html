About Me

I'm a Mechanical Engineering student at IIT Bombay (Class of 2024) with a passion for building things — from hardware prototypes to digital content. In my second year, I joined a startup as a graphic design intern, but quickly transitioned into product development. I led the mechanical and product design of an IoT-based startup, collaborating closely with a small team to design enclosures, assist with PCB integration, and prototype functional hardware. That hands-on experience sparked my love for products that blend engineering, design, and practical problem-solving.

Alongside this, I was actively involved in tech teams during my third and fourth years — serving as Vice President of Marketing for Team Rakshak and Head of Design for Inter IIT Tech 12.0. In both roles, I managed design elements like team apparel, logos, and branding, and contributed to securing sponsorships. These leadership experiences helped me develop teamwork, communication, and project management skills within competitive technical environments.

Outside the classroom, I work as a freelancer and YouTuber, creating content on tech, editing videos, and building custom PC setups. I’m especially interested in the intersection of hardware, tech culture, and creative media. I’m currently exploring opportunities where I can contribute to real-world projects that combine engineering, design, and digital creativity.
